What I Do

I work as a freelance sound system engineer and sound technician, supporting productions where system reliability and predictable results matter.

My work typically includes PA system design and deployment, system tuning and alignment, coverage optimization, and on-site troubleshooting — always adapting to the needs of each production.

Experience & Approach

I’ve spent 25+ years working in live sound across tours, festivals, theatres, and arena-scale events. I’m used to stepping into existing productions in many different roles of the audio team, understanding constraints quickly, and delivering systems that support the show rather than draw attention to themselves.

Calm, practical, and focused — no unnecessary changes, just solutions that work even under pressure.

Working Model

Freelance / project-based work in Finland, Europe, and internationally. Short-term, long-term, and drop-in system roles.
